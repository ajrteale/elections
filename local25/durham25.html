Durham County election results - 1 May 2025

The verification of the ballot boxes for the County Council elections will start from 10.00pm on Thursday 1 May 2025 at Consett Leisure Centre, Consett and Spennymoor Leisure Centre, Spennymoor. The counting of the County Council ballot papers will commence from 10.00am on Friday 2 May 2025 at Consett Leisure Centre, Consett and Spennymoor Leisure Centre, Spennymoor.
