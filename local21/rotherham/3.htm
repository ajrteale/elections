Aston and Todwick ward

Election of a Borough Councillor for Aston and Todwick ward on Thursday 6 May 2021

I, Stuart Fletcher, being the Deputy Returning Officer at this election, do hereby give notice that the number of votes recorded for each Candidate is as follows:

Name of Candidate Description (if any) Number of Votes*
BACON Joshua Conservative Party Candidate 1,103 ELECTED
BARKER Aaron Conservative Party Candidate 1,025 ELECTED
BARKER Louisa Kathryn The Green Party 250
CLARKE Amanda Marie Labour Party 783
FIELDS Joshua Lennon Trade Unionist and Socialist Coalition 68
HALLAM-WALL Chris Liberal Democrats 141
RATCLIFFE Harry Rotherham Democratic Party 143
TAYLOR Robert Paul Labour Party 905

* If elected the word 'Elected' appears against the number of votes.

The number of ballot papers rejected was as follows:

Number of ballot papers

(number of rejected votes)
A Want of official mark 0 (0)
B Voting for more candidates than the voter is entitled to vote for 0 (0)
C Writing or mark by which the voter can be identified 0 (0)
D Unmarked or void for uncertainty 11 (22)
E Rejected in part 0 (0)
Total 11 (22)

Vacant seats

2

Electorate

 7,057

Ballot papers issued

2,430

Turnout

34.43%

I do hereby declare that the said Joshua BACON and Aaron BARKER duly elected Councillors for the Aston and Todwick ward.

Sharon Kemp

Returning Officer
