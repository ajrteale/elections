Local election results

Salford City Council comprises 20 electoral wards with three councillors representing each ward.

The city council is controlled by the Labour party, the political composition of the city council is:

Party Councillors
Labour 52
Conservative 7
Liberal Democrat 1

The full details of local election results can be seen below.

Terms of office for councillors

The terms of office of the three newly elected councillors is determined by the amount of votes received by each candidate.

  • The candidate with the highest amount of votes will be elected to serve a three year term until May 2024
  • The candidate with the second highest amount of votes will be elected to serve a two year term of office until May 2023
  • The candidate with the third highest amount of votes will be elected to serve a one year term of office until May 2022
