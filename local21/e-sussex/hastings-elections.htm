Election of County Councillors

Ashdown and Conquest Division

I, the undersigned, being the Returning Officer at the election for Councillors for the Ashdown and Conquest Division, held on the 6th May 2021, hereby give notice that the number of votes recorded for each candidate is as follows:

Poll results
Candidate Description Number of recorded votes
Martin Paul Griffiths Liberal Democrats 235
Peter Pragnell Conservative Party 1609
Judy Rogers Labour Party 629
Christopher Saunders Green Party 219
Number of ballot papers rejected - 18

I hereby declare that the said Peter Pragnell (The Conservative Party) is the duly elected Councillor for the said Division.

Jane Hartnell
Returning Officer
