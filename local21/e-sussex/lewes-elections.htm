Lewes division

Deputy Returning Officer's announcement

The results for the Lewes division are as follows:

Poll results
Candidate Description Number of votes
David John Charles Charnock Conservative Party 542
Peter Hambly Labour Party 763
Wendy Maples Green Party 2030
Catherine Elizabeth Wood Liberal Democrats 1345

Accordingly, Wendy Maples has been elected as a County Councillor for the Lewes division to hold office until the year 2025.
