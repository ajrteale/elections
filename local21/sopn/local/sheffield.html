    Notice of Election

    Notice is hereby given that

    1. Elections are to be held for Councillors for the under mentioned Wards.
    2. The number of Councillors to be elected is:
      Park & Arbourthorne 1
      Beauchief & Greenhill 1
      Beighton 1
      Birley 1
      Broomhill & Sharrow Vale 1
      Burngreave 1
      City 1
      Crookes & Crosspool 1
      Darnall 1
      Dore & Totley 1
      East Ecclesfield 1
      Ecclesall 1
      Firth Park 1
      Fulwood 1
      Gleadless Valley 1
      Graves Park 1
      Hillsborough 1
      Manor Castle 1
      Mosborough 1
      Nether Edge & Sharrow 1
      Richmond 2
      Shiregreen & Brightside 1
      Southey 1
      Stannington 1
      Stocksbridge & Upper Don 1
      Walkley 1
      West Ecclesfield 1
      Woodhouse 1
    3. Nomination papers may be obtained, during normal office hours, from the Returning Officer at the address shown below.
    4. Completed nomination papers must be delivered to the Returning Officer, Sheffield City Council, Town Hall, Pinstone Street, S1 2HH on any day after the date of this notice, during normal office hours but Not later than 4pm on - Thursday, 8 April 2021.
    5. If the election is contested, the poll will take place on Thursday, 6 May 2021 between the hours of 07:00 and 22:00.
    6. If you are not already registered an application to register must reach the Electoral Registration Officer by Monday, 19 April 2021 if it is to be effective for this election.
    7. Applications, amendments or cancellations of postal votes must reach the Electoral Registration Officer at the address shown below by 5pm on Tuesday, 20 April 2021 if they are to be effective for this election.
    8. New applications to vote by proxy must be received by the Electoral Registration Officer by 5pm on Tuesday, 27 April 2021. Requests for changes to existing proxy vote arrangements and cancellations must reach the Electoral Registration Officer by 5pm on Tuesday 20 April 2021.
    9. After 5pm on Tuesday, 27 April 2021 applications to vote by emergency proxy at this election for medical or work/service reasons must reach the Electoral Registration Officer by 5pm on Thursday, 6 May 2021.

    Dated Tuesday 23 March 2021. Kate Josephs, Returning Officer.

    Printed and Published by The Returning Officer, Town hall, Pinstone Street, Sheffield S1 2HH
