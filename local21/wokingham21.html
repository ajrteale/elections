Borough Council Election results 2021

Results of Borough Council elections on Thursday 6 May

For results of the Police and Crime Commissioner election in the Thames Valley region see Police and Crime Commissioner Thames Valley region election results 2021 (PDF document) or visit Cherwell District Council Police and Crime Commissioner election results website page.

For results of Wokingham Borough Town and Council elections on Thursday 6 May visit Town and Parish Council Election results 2021 website page.

The make up of Wokingham Borough Council is now:

  • Conservatives - 31
  • Liberal Democrats - 18
  • Labour - 3
  • Independent - 2
