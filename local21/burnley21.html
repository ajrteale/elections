Election Results 2021

The results for the Burnley Borough Council elections held on 6th May 2021 are as follows below. These results lead to the political make up of the council being:

  • Labour (18)
  • Conservatives (9)
  • Liberal Democrats (8)
  • Burnley and Padiham Independent Party (5)
  • Greens (5)

Lancashire County Council – the results for the six divisions in our borough are at the bottom of this page.

The Lancashire Police and Crime Commissioner (PCC) results are available on their website.

The Borough of Burnley results were as follows:

First preference/first count

  • BARKER, Mark James (Reform UK) – 1480 votes
  • DARBY, Neil (Liberal Democrats) – 3132 votes
  • GRUNSHAW, Clive (Labour and Co-operative Party) – 9137 votes
  • SNOWDEN, Andrew James (The Conservative Party Candidate) – 8084 votes
  • Rejected votes – 1122

Second preference/second count

  • GRUNSHAW, Clive (Labour and Co-operative Party) – 1410 votes
  • SNOWDEN, Andrew James (The COnservative Party Canddiate) – 1351 votes
  • Rejected votes – 574

Lancashire County Council Elections 2021

Elections to Lancashire County Council were held on Thursday 6th May 2021.

Results in our borough are as follows, the County Councillors elected from Burnley electoral divisions were:

  • (Central East) – Sobia Malik (Labour)
  • (Central West) – Andrew Fewings (Green)
  • (North East) – Usman Arif (Labour)
  • (Rural) – Cosima Towneley (Conservative)
  • (South West) – Lian Pate (Labour)
  • (Padiham and Burnley West) – Alan Hosker (Conservative)

All Lancashire’s results are available on Lancashire County Council’s website.
