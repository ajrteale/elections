Elections 2021

You can view our Twitter feed to see how the 2021 election results were announced (you don't have to join Twitter to view the feed).

The election count and result announcements took place across two halls at Southend Leisure and Tennis Centre this year, with western wards and St Luke’s being counted in the tennis courts area, and eastern and central wards counted in the main sports hall.
