Norwich City Council election results - 6 May 2021

On Thursday 6 May residents went to the polls to elect a new city councillor in each of the 13 wards in the Norwich boundary.

The Sewell ward election was postponed due to the death of one of the candidates and has been rearranged for 17 June 2021.

The results of the city council local election were declared on Saturday 8 May 2021.
