Four by-elections on 24th April 2025:

Glenrothes Central and Thornton

Fife council, Scotland; caused by the death of Scottish National Party councillor Ross Vettraino.

Thatcham North East

West Berkshire council; caused by the resignation of Liberal Democrat councillor Lee Dillon.

Marine

Arun council, West Sussex; caused by the death of independent councillor Jim Brooks.

St John's

Suffolk county council; caused by the resignation of Labour councillor Sarah Adams.
