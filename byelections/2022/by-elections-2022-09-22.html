Four by-elections on 22nd September 2022:

Maresfield

Wealden council, West Sussex; caused by the resignation of Conservative councillor Peter Roundell.

Sherbourne

Coventry council, West Midlands; caused by the death of Labour councillor Seamus Walsh.

Bentilee and Ubberley

Stoke-on-Trent council, Staffordshire; caused by the resignation of Labour councillor Stephen Funnell.

Llanuwchllyn

Gwynedd council, North Wales; caused by the disqualification of Plaid Cymru councillor Alun Evans, who failed to sign his declaration of acceptance of office.
