Four by-elections on 14th April 2022:

West Auckland

Durham council; caused by the death of Conservative councillor Mark Roberts.

Heybridge West

Maldon council, Essex; caused by the disqualification of independent councillor Chrisy Morris, who was sentenced to eight months imprisonment, suspended, for breaching a non-molestation order.

Bisley and West End

Surrey Heath council; caused by the resignation of Conservative councillor David Mansfield.

Brockworth East

Tewkesbury council, Gloucestershire; caused by the resignation of independent councillor Louise Gerrard.
