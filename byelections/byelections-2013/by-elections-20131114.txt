By-elections on 14th November 2013:

BADDELEY, MILTON AND NORTON, Stoke-on-Trent city council; caused by the resignation of former Labour councillor Andrew Lilley.  Lilley, who was formerly treasurer of the Stoke-on-Trent Central branch of the Labour Party, now is serving a 16-month prison sentence for helping himself to £10,000 of party funds.

SEATON, Allerdale district council, Cumbria and SEATON, Cumbria county council; both caused by the death of Independent councillor Trevor Fee, who sat on both councils, at the age of 57.

WINTON EAST, Bournemouth council; caused by the resignation of Conservative councillor Anniina Davie.
