Four by-elections on 21st May 2026:

Castle

Lancaster council; caused by the resignation of Green Party councillor Izzy Metcalf-Riener.

Kirkham

Fylde council, Lancashire; caused by the resignation of independent councillor Damian Buckley.

Alfrick, Leigh and Rushwick

Malvern Hills council, Worcestershire; caused by the resignation of Malvern Hills Independent councillor Peter Whatley.

Bridport

Dorset council; caused by the death of Liberal Democrat councillor Dave Bolwell.
