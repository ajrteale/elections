Four by-elections on Thursday 18th January 2018:

<hr/>

<h2>Hulton</h2>

<hr/>

<h2>Newport Pagnell North and Hanslope</h2>

Milton Keynes council, Buckinghamshire; caused by the death of Conservative councillor Jeannette Green.

<hr/>

<h2>Throop and Muscliff</h2>

Bournemouth council, Dorset; caused by the resignation of independent councillor Anne Rey who is retiring to Spain.

<hr/>

<h2>Downhall and Rawreth</h2>

Rochford council, Essex; caused by the death of Liberal Democrat councillor Chris Black.
