Three by-elections on 12th April 2018, all due to resignations:

<h2>St Olaves</h2>

St Edmundsbury council; caused by the resignation of Labour councillor Bob Cockle.

<hr/>

<h2>Middleton Cheney</h2>

South Northamptonshire council; caused by the resignation of Conservative councillor Judith Baxter.

<hr/>

<h2>Rogate</h2>

Chichester council, West Sussex; caused by the resignation of Conservative councillor Gillian Keegan, who is now the MP for Chichester.
