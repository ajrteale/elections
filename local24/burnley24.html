Election results 2024

The results of the borough and Lancashire Police and Crime Commissioner elections held on 2nd May 2024 were as follows.

Lancashire Police and Crime Commissioner (PCC)

The overall result for the whole of Lancashire

Borough of Burnley results

These results below are for the borough of Burnley only.

DARBY, Neil (Liberal Democrats) 4096

GRIMSHAW, Clive (Labour and Co-operative Party) 8140

SNOWDEN, Andrew (Conservative and Unionist Party) 5897

Lancashire results

The overall results for Lancashire were:

DARBY, Neil (Liberal Democrats) 51,252 votes

GRUNSHAW, Clive (Labour and Co-operative Party) 135,638 (ELECTED)

SNOWDEN, Andrew Conservative and Unionist Party) 101,281

Burnley Borough Council

For the results see below.

The overall turnout across the borough was 30%.

The political make-up of the council is as follows:

  • Labour 15 seats (previously 11)
  • Independent 10 (11)
  • Conservatives 8 (8)
  • Liberal Democrats 7 (7)
  • Greens 5 (7)
