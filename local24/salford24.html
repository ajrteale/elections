Election results: 2 May 2024

The Local Government elections, Salford City Mayor election and Greater Manchester Mayor election took place on Thursday 2 May 2024.

Salford City Mayor election results

The total number of votes given for each candidate was as follows:

Candidate Party Votes cast
Jillian Rose Collinson The Conservative Party Candidate 10,930
Paul Dennett Labour Party 30,753
Sally Griffiths Trade Unionist and Socialist Coalition 2,681
David Jones Green Party 5,623
  • Elected: Paul Dennett
  • Electorate: 193,084
  • Total number of votes cast: 49,987
  • Turnout percentage: 25.89%

The number of ballot papers rejected was as follows:

  • Want of official mark: 0
  • Voting for more than one candidate: 87
  • Writing or mark by which voter could be identified: 2
  • Unmarked or void for uncertainty: 601

Total number of rejected ballot papers: 690
