Chapter 16  South Western Unitaries

There were no elections this year in Bath and North East Somerset, Bournemouth, North Somerset, Poole, South Gloucestershire and Torbay, which hold whole council elections and whose next elections will be in 2007.

There were also no elections this year in Bristol, which elects by thirds but misses this year in the electoral cycle.
