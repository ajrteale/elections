Election results

The Local Government election took place on Thursday 7 May 2026.

Last updated: 3.16am (Cadishead and Lower Irlam)

Total turnout: 34.33%

Total electorate: 197,471

Composition of the council after the election

Salford City Council comprises 20 electoral wards with three councillors representing each ward.

The city council is controlled by the Labour party, the political composition of the city council is:

Party Councillors
Labour 34
Reform UK 14
Conservative 6
Green Party 3
Liberal Democrat 1
Independent 1
Democratic Socialist Party 1

About the count

The count for the local elections (one councillor for each of the 20 wards except for Cadishead and Lower Irlam where there is another vacancy, so 21 councillors will be elected) took place overnight on Thursday 7 May 2026 at the Salford Community Stadium. You can watch a live stream of the count and results below.
