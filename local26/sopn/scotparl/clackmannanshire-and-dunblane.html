Statement of Persons Nominated and Notice of Poll - Scottish Parliament Election 2026

Clackmannanshire and Dunblane Constituency

A poll will be held on 7 May 2026 between 7am and 10pm.

The following people have been or stand nominated for election as a member of the Scottish Parliament for the above constituency. Those who no longer stand nominated are listed, but will have a comment in the right hand column.

Name of candidate Description of candidate (if any) If candidate no longer nominated,reason why
BROWN, Keith James                    Scottish National Party (SNP)  
COLLIER, Mike Reform UK  
COMRIE, Eva Margaret Alliance to Liberate Scotland  
GRAHAME, Suzanne Scottish Labour Party  
PATTLE, Sally Scottish Liberal Democrats  
SCACCHI, Luca Independent  
STEWART, Alexander Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party  


Nikki Bridle, Constituency Returning Officer
1 April 2026
