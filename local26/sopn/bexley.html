Local Elections on 7 May 2026

  • There will be local elections on 7 May 2026 to elect Councillors to all 17 wards in the London Borough of Bexley
  • The hours of polling will be between 7am and 10pm
  • If you need to check if you are registered to vote, have an absent vote in place, or for any other election enquiries, please contact Electoral Services using the details below
  • To find your polling station, please use the search tool at: Where is my polling station?
  • You will need to bring photo ID with you to vote in person at your polling station
  • A full list of acceptable forms of photo ID can be found at: How to vote: Photo ID you'll need
  • The notice on the next page a statutory notice of election
  • The notice on the next page includes information on the number of vacancies per ward
  • The notice on the next page also includes the deadlines to register to vote, apply for and amend absent vote arrangements, and apply for voter ID
