Local Election results 2026

Find out all the results for the St Helens Borough Council Local Elections 2026 as they come in here.

Article date: Friday 8th May

Today, Friday 8th May, the votes will be counted for the St Helens Borough Council Local Elections 2026 with 46 seats being decided today. The process sees ballot papers verified first before the count takes place. The turnout figure for the election is 38.5 per cent with 51,751 votes cast yesterday at the polls.
