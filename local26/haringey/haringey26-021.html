Woodside ward

Local election results

Confirmed local election results for Woodside ward, held on Thursday 7 May:

  • BOZDOGAN, Hasret (Labour Party) – votes: 1,337, elected: Yes
  • DAS NEVES, Lucia (Labour Party) – votes: 1,434, elected: Yes
  • FURERAJ, Mikeleno (Conservative Party) – votes: 264
  • HAND, Rita (Conservative Party) – votes: 316
  • HYMAS, Tammy (Green Party) – votes: 1,394, elected: Yes
  • ISILAR-GOSLING, Marsha (Liberal Democrats) – votes: 388
  • IYNGKARAN, Thayahlan (Labour Party) – votes: 1,151
  • JUKES, Hayley Nicola (Green Party) – votes: 1,334
  • MIHELE, Ciprian Moises (Reform UK) – votes: 240
  • MOHAMMED, Sumrah Paras (Green Party) – votes: 1,158
  • O'SHEA, Neil (Conservative Party) – votes: 246
  • PRICE, Ruth (Reform UK) – votes: 266
  • SCOTT, Nigel Peter (Liberal Democrats) – votes: 355
  • SMITH, Keshon (Liberal Democrats) – votes: 293
  • WEI, Da (Reform UK) – votes: 200

Number of ward councillors elected: 3
Number of rejected papers: 16
Turnout: 3,572 (36.28%)
Ward electorate: 9,847
