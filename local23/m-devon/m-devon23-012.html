Sandford & Creedy - District Election Results

DECLARATION OF RESULT OF POLL

Mid Devon Election of a District Councillor for Sandford & Creedy on Thursday 4 May 2023.

I, Stephen Walford, being the Deputy Returning Officer at the above election, do hereby give notice that the number of votes recorded for each candidate at the said election is as follows:

Name of Candidate Description (if any) Number of Votes*
MONRO, Alexander William [Alex] Conservative Party Candidate 517
JENKINS, Mark Anthony [Mark] Green Party 576(Elected)
SQUIRES, Margaret Edith  Conservative Party Candidate 575
TUFFIN, Helen Marie Green Party 632 (Elected)

* If elected the word 'Elected' appears against the number of votes.

 

The number of ballot papers rejected was as follows: Number of ballot papers
A - want of an official mark  2
B - voting for more Candidates than voter was entitled to - 
C - writing or mark by which voter could be identified - 
D - being unmarked or wholly void for uncertainty 12 
E - rejected in part - 
Total 14 

Vacant Seats: 2

Electorate: 2,840

Ballot Papers Issued: 1,238

Turnout: 43.6%

And I do hereby declare that HELEN TUFFIN and MARK JENKINS are duly elected.

Dated: 5 May 2023
Stephen Walford, Deputy Returning Officer
