Counting will take place on Friday 5 May 2023, the results will then be announced on this page.

Further information

Key deadlines

  • Register to vote - 12am midnight on Monday 17 April 2023
  • Register for postal votes - 5pm on Tuesday 18 April
  • Register for proxy votes - 5pm on Tuesday 25 April
  • Voter Authority Certificates deadline - 5pm on Tuesday 25 April

Polling stations

Your poll card will tell you which polling station you need to go to. As a result of the boundary changes, your polling station may have changed. Nearer the time, you can use our Find your nearest polling station page.

Poll cards

All registered electors will receive a poll card by the end of March. From May 2023, poll cards will be A4 size to list the acceptable forms of photo ID. You will receive it in an envelope. If you have not received a card by 7 April it may mean you are not registered. If you have moved in early 2023, you need to make sure you re-register at your new address.

Postal votes

Portal votes will be issued as soon as possible after the close of nominations. These are sent via first-class post. If you applied near the deadline, these will be posted by the Friday before polling day.

Proxy voting

If you have appointed a proxy to vote on your behalf, you will both receive a card. Your proxy must vote at your allocated polling station.
