Local election results May 2023

  1. Home
  2. About Us

  4. Local election results May 2023

Local elections to appoint City of York councillors for council wards in York were held on Thursday 4 May 2023. Details of elected candidates, announced by the Returning Officer, on Friday 5 May, are shown on this page.

The map shows which political parties are represented in each ward (numbers on the map relate to the ward list):

  • Labour Party (red)
  • Liberal Democrat Party (yellow)
  • Conservative party (blue)
  • Independents (grey)
  • Yorkshire Party (pale blue)
  • Green Party (green)
  •  Trade Unionist and Socialist Coalition (pink)
