Voting information

  • Register to vote on the GOV.UK website if you haven't already - the deadline to register to vote in the May 2023 elections was Monday 17 April 2023

  • If you would prefer not to go to the polling station, you can apply for a postal vote or a proxy vote. You must be registered to vote to apply. If you already have a postal or proxy application in place, you don't need to apply again.

  • The hours of poll were 7am to 10pm on Thursday 4 May 2023. Details of your polling station may have changed for this election. You may need to vote at a different location or in a different building. Please check your poll card for the location of your polling station.

  • The Elections Act 2022 requires you to bring photographic ID with you to the polling station. You can find more information about this on our Voter ID page.

Wards and polling stations

To find which ward or parish council area you live in, please check our interactive map (opens Peterborough Arcgis system). Please go to the 'council boundaries' drop down where you can select options to view your ward, boundary, parish council area, nearest polling station etc.

Your poll card also shows your ward and polling station.

Contact

If you have any queries relating to local or parish council elections please email electoralservices@peterborough.gov.uk or visit the Electoral Commission webpages for local elections or parish council elections.
