Bin collections

Grey refuse bins and the red recycling bins will be collected on alternate weeks. We only collect rubbish in bins where the lids are fully closed.

Summary of the elections results:

Following the elections the make up of the council is:

21 Conservatives
18 Labour
3 Liberal democrats
