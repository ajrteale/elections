      Local Council (District) elections

      Stockton-on-Tees has 56 Councillors who represent the 27 wards across the Borough.

      Local elections are held once every 4 years, on the first Thursday of May.

      Fairfield By-Election September 2024

      Notice has been given that a casual vacancy exists in the office of Borough Councillor for the Fairfield Ward. You can find out all of the information on this election on the Fairfield By-Election web page.
