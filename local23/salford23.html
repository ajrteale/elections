Election results

New government requirement to show ID at polling stations

The Local Government elections will take place on Thursday 4 May 2023.

The count for the local election took place at the AJ Bell Stadium, 1 Stadium Way, Eccles, M30 7EY.

The city council is controlled by the Labour party, the political composition of the city council is now:

Party Councillors
Labour 49
Conservative 8
Liberal Democrat 2
Independent 1
