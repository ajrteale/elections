Norwich City Council election results - 4 May 2023

The count was held on Friday 5 May 2023, at The Halls, Norwich.

Total eligible electorate: 101,929

Overall percentage turnout: 35.8

Eligible in-person voters who did not have approved voter ID, and did not return with the correct ID, therefore could not be issued with a ballot paper: 68

Eligible in-person voters who did not arrive with approved voter ID but subsequently returned with approved ID and were issued with a ballot paper: 116
