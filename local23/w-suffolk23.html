West Suffolk Council election results 4 May 2023

The verification of ballot papers, which is where we count the total number of ballot papers, took place at Bury St Edmunds Leisure Centre as soon as the poll closed at 10pm on 4 May.

The election count, which is where the ballot papers are sorted by candidate and results declared, took place at Bury St Edmunds Leisure Centre on Friday 5 May from 9am.

The results of the election were published on this page once they had been declared at the count on Friday 5 May 2023. Results were also posted as they happened on the council’s Twitter feed @West_Suffolk

Seats by party or independent - results

This was updated to show the running total of the number of seats for each party or independent candidates, as the results were added to the website

There were 64 seats on West Suffolk Council up for election. For a party or group to have overall control of the council they must have 33 seats.
