Electoral Registers

Because of minor changes to Willingdon and Jevington Parish Ward boundaries and consequential changes to District Ward Boundaries in that area, a revised electoral register will be published on 1 March to reflect those changes. The polling district letters will remain the same but all elector electoral numbers will change.  It is this March register that must be used for the nominations process (not the one published in December 2022).

The 1 March register will be distributed to registered political parties in early March. Prospective candidates can request a copy of the revised register, but in accordance with the legislation, it cannot be distributed to prospective candidates until the last date for publishing the Notice of Election which is Monday 27 March 2023.

However, the Elections Team will be happy to provide and confirm electoral numbers for prospective candidates by telephone (01892 602407) from 1 March onwards.
