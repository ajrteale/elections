Published May 6, 2016 at 13:18

These are the results and vote number in the Rossendale Local Elections, May 2016;

Cribden Ward

Andrea FLETCHER (Labour Party) – 542 votes (ELECTED) Labour HOLD

Michael PICKUP (UK Independence Party) – 152 votes

Jenny RIGBY (Conservative Party) – 391 votes

Hilary Jane WHITEHEAD (Green Party) – 71 votes

Ward turnout = 41.60%

Eden Ward

Janice Beverley JOHNSON (Labour Party) – 549 votes – (ELECTED) – Labour GAIN

Mischa MOCKETT (Conservative Party) – 492 votes

Ward Turnout = 36.32%

Facit & Shawforth Ward

Tom ALDRED (Labour Party) – 381 votes (ELECTED) – Labour GAIN

Max Alexander LAKE (UK Independence Party) – 200 votes

Dayne POWELL (Conservative Party) – 304 votes

Ward Turnout = 31.82%

Goodshaw Ward

Ronald Broadley ASHWORTH (UK Independence Party) – 122 votes

Dorothy FARRINGTON (Labour Party) – 709 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Lynn TATT (Conservative Party) – 378

Ward Turnout = 38.60%

Greenfield Ward

Lisa Michelle BLOOR (Labour Party) – 584 votes

Val ROBERTS (Conservative Party) – 693 votes (ELECTED) – Conservative Hold

Alan WEAVER (UK Independence Party) – 212 votes

Ward Turnout = 34.20%

Greensclough Ward

Barbara Marion ASHWORTH (Labour Party) – 839 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Mark MILLS (Conservative Party) – 630 votes

Ward Turnout = 34.10%

Hareholme Ward

Clive BALCHIN (Independent) – 547 votes

David Alexander FOXCROFT (Conservative Party) – 477 votes

Andy MACNAE (Labour Party) – 615 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Ward Turnout = 39.96%

Helmshore Ward

Noel William O’BRIEN (Independent) – 236 votes

David STANSFIELD (Conservative Party) – 1003 votes (ELECTED) – Conservative HOLD

Sam STUART-BOOTH (Labour Party) – 700 votes

Ward Turnout = 40.54%

Irwell Ward

Kevin Alastair BRYAN (National Front) – 128 votes

Laura-Beth THOMPSON (Conservative Party) – 462 votes

Andrew WALMSLEY (Labour Party) – 661 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Ward Turnout = 30.62%

Longholme Ward

Gary BARNES (UK Independence Party) – 262 votes

Thomas Alfred BLACKBURN (Conservative Party) – 500 votes

Steve HUGHES (Labour Party) – 768 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Ward Turnout = 36.83%

Whitewell Ward

Pam BROMLEY (Labour Party) – 651 votes (ELECTED) – Labour GAIN

Sarah Elizabeth COLLINGE (Conservative Party) – 607 votes

John David PAYNE (Green Party) – 137 votes

Ward Turnout = 33.43%

Worsley Ward

Naomi ADAMS (Conservative Party) –  418 votes

Granville BARKER (UK Independence Party) – 265 votes

Marilyn Winifred Anne PROCTER (Labour Party) – 733 votes (ELECTED) – Labour HOLD

Ward Turnout = 33.81%

 

Overall Local Election Turnout = 35.92%

PCC Election Turnout = 33.97%
