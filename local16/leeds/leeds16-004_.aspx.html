Main Body Text
The results of the Leeds City Council election held on the 5th of May 2016 are available on this page. Click on the link for each ward to find out who was elected.

The current political composition of the council is:
  • Labour - 63
  • Conservative - 19
  • Liberal Democrat - 9
  • Morley Borough Independents - 5
  • Green Party - 3

For further information please visit our councillors and democracy page​.

​
This page will be live for two months. After this, results can be found on Leeds Data Mill​ along with those from previous elections.
