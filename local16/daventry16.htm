Daventry District Council election results May 2016

The count for the 2016 Daventry District Council elections was held from 10pm on Thursday 5 May at Daventry Leisure Centre. A total of 12 seats on the Council were up for election. The results are published below.
The political make-up of the Council following the results is 30 Conservative, 3 Labour, 2 UKIP and 1 Lib Dem.
