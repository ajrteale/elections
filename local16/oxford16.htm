There were elections across all wards for Oxford City Council on Thursday 5 May 2016. Each ward elected one councillor to serve a four-year term, until May 2020.

There were also elections for parish councils (in two parts of the city) and the Police and Crime Commissioner for Thames Valley Police. Results of the Police and Crime Commissioner election can be downloaded from our Election results pages.

Oxford City Council Election Results - 5 May 2016

The count for the Oxford City Council elections started at 9.30am on Friday 6 May 2016. Results are published below. 

The political make-up of the council following the election is:

  • Labour - 35 councillors
  • Lib Dem - 8 councillors
  • Green - 4 councillors
  • Independent - 1 councillor
