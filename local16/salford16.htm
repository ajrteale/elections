Election results: 5 May 2016

The City Mayor and local elections took place on Thursday 5 May 2016.  

City Mayor and local election results 

The City Mayor and local election results held on Thursday 5 May 2016 are below, * indicates a sitting councillor. 

We also published the results on Facebook and Twitter. 

City Mayor election results 

Labour candidate Paul Dennett is Salford City Council’s new City Mayor.

Candidate name Party Total votes received
DENNETT, Paul Labour 28,332
GARRIDO, Robin Conservative 14,484
HAMMOND, Owen Martin UK Independence Party (UKIP) 8,668
OLSEN, Wendy Kay Green 4,158
