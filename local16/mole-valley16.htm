Surrey Police and Crime Commissioner Results

All local authorities throughout Surrey ran a Police and Crime Commissioner election in their areas on 5 May.

The results of each area were announced by the Police Area Returning Officer at Reigate and Banstead Borough Council (RBBC) on Friday 6 May 2016.

David John Munro, Conservative Party candidate, was elected.

You can view the results of the Surrey PCC election on the RBBC website: http://www.reigate-banstead.gov.uk/pccelection

The Police and Crime Commissioner is responsible for setting the strategic objectives for policing in Surrey.

Local Advisory Poll

On 5 May 2016, a Local Advisory Poll was held in Dorking, which asked whether a Parish Council should be established in the town.

The result for this Poll was as follows:

Question: Should a Parish Council be established for Dorking?

Votes Recorded
Yes1912
No1971

These figures, together with any other consultation responses received as part of the Community Governance Review, will be reported to a meeting of Mole Valley District Council for its consideration in deciding whether or not to approve the establishment of a Parish Council for Dorking.
