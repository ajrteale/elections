District (Local) election


Page Content
  • Download the notice of election

District Council elections will take place on 5 May 2016. One seat in each of our 21 wards will be up for election. Nominations open on Wednesday, 30 March and close at 4pm on Thursday, 7 April. Information about who is standing for election will be available on this page soon after the close of nominations.

Poll cards will be delivered to voters in the district from Wednesday, 30 March. Anyone who does not receive a polling card should check that they are registered by contacting Electoral Services on 01924 305023. The deadline to register to vote in May is Monday, 18 April.

  • More information about registering to vote

You can vote in person, by post or by proxy. The deadline to apply for a postal vote is 5pm on Tuesday, 19 April. The deadline to vote by proxy is 5pm on Tuesday, 26 April.

  • More information about voting by post or proxy
