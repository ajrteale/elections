Scottish Parliamentary elections 2016

Notice of Poll & Statement of Persons and Parties Nominated

The following people have been or stand nominated for election as a member of the Scottish Parliament for:

Strathkelvin and  Bearsden Constituency:

  • Katy Gordon Scottish Liberal Democrat Party
  • Rona Mackay Scottish National Party (SNP)
  • Margaret McCarthy Scottish Labour Party
  • Andrew Polson Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party

West of Scotland Region:

  • For the West of Scotland Region Notice of Poll view our documents section.  

Clydebank and Milngavie Constituency

Residents who live in the local wards of Milngavie and Bearsden North contribute to the Clydebank & Milngavie Scottish Parliamentary Constituency. The Returning Officer is the Chief Executive of West Dunbartonshire Council who will provide all relevant election materials including, candidate nomination packs, poll cards, postal votes etc 
  • Frank Bowles Scottish Liberal Democrats
  • Gail Casey Scottish Labour Party
  • Maurice Golden Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party
  • Gil Paterson Scottish National Party (SNP)

 

Scottish Parliament Election - Notice of Election - Strathkelvin and Bearsden Constituency 

An election is to be held for a Constituency Member to serve in the Scottish Parliament for the Strathkelvin & Bearsden constituency. If the election is contested the poll will take place on Thursday 5 May 2016. The Notice of Election provides more information particularly for potential candidates 
 

Scottish Parliament Election - Notice of Election - West of Scotland Region

An election is to be held for 7 members to serve in the Scottish Parliament for the West Scotland Region. If the election is contested the poll will take place on Thursday 5 May 2016. The Regional Returning Officer is the Chief Executive of Renfrewshire Council, Sandra Black and she has provided this Notice of Election. The West Scotland Region covers the following Council areas:- East Dunbartonshire, West Dunbartonshire, Renfrewshire, East Renfrewshire, Inverclyde and North Ayrshire
 

Scottish Parliament Election - Notice of Election - Clydebank and Milngavie Constituency

Residents who live in the local wards of Milngavie and Bearsden North contribute to the Clydebank & Milngavie Scottish Parliamentary Constituency. The Returning Officer is the Chief Executive of West Dunbartonshire Council who will provide all relevant election materials including, candidate nomination packs, poll cards, postal votes etc 

An election is to be held for a Constituency Member to serve in the Scottish Parliament for the Clydebank & Milngavie constituency. If the election is contested the poll will take place on Thursday 5 May 2016. The Notice of Election provides more information particularly for potential candidates.
