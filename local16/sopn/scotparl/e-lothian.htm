Constituency Candidates

The following four candidates stand nominated for election for the East Lothian constituency:

  • GRAY, Iain Cumming - Scottish Labour Party
  • HAMILTON, Rachael - Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party
  • JOHNSTON-SMITH, DJ - Scottish National Party (SNP)
  • SPENCER, Ettie - Scottish Liberal Democrats

Regional Party Lists

These lists are available on the Dumfries and Galloway Council website.
