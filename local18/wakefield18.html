Local Election results 2018

Page Content

​Wakefield Council remains in overall Labour control

Turnout: 26.86%

 

Labour

  • held 15 of 17 defended seats
  • lost two seats to the Conservatives
  • gained one seat from an Independent 

The Conservatives

  • held their defended seat
  • gained two seats from Labour, one seat from UKIP and one seat from an Independent

UKIP

  • lost their defended seat

Independents

  • lost both defended seats, one to Labour and one to The Conservatives

The make-up of the Council is:

  • Labour 52 seats
  • Conservative 11 seats
  • UKIP 0 seats
  • Independent 0 seats
