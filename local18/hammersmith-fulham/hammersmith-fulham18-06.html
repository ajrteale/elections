2018 Fulham Reach ward result

Result of the election for three councillors in Fulham Reach ward held on Thursday 3 May 2018.

The results were declared at 4.02am on Friday 4 May and the turnoutÂ was 41.38%.

The candidates duly elected to serve as councillors for Fulham Reach ward were:

  • Christabel Laura Cooper - Labour
  • Ian Graeme Cassidy - Labour
  • Guy Wyndham Vincent - Labour
