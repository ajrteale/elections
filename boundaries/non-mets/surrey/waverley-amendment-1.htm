STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2000 No. 3366

LOCAL GOVERNMENT, ENGLAND

The Borough of Waverley (Electoral Changes) (Amendment) Order 2000

  Made 21st December 2000 
  Coming into force in accordance with article 1(2)

The Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions, in exercise of the powers conferred on him by sections 17 and 26 of the Local Government Act 1992[1], and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, hereby makes the following Order:

Citation and commencement
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Borough of Waverley (Electoral Changes) (Amendment) Order 2000.

    (2) This Order shall come into force on the day after that on which it is made.

Amendment of Order
     2. The Borough of Waverley (Electoral Changes) Order 1999[2] is amended, in Schedule 1, column (3), - 

    (a) by the insertion, in respect of the column (1) entry "Godalming Binscombe", of the numeral "2";

    (b) by the substitution - 

      (i) for the numeral "3", of the numeral "2", in respect of the column (1) entry "Haslemere East and Grayswood"; and

      (ii) for the numeral "2", of the numeral "3", in respect of the column (1) entry "Hindhead".



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions


Beverley Hughes
Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State, Department of the Environment, Transport and the Regions

21st December 2000


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order corrects errors in the Borough of Waverley (Electoral Changes) Order 1999, which has effect in relation to local government elections to be held on and after 1st May 2003. The amendments provide for two councillors to be elected for each of the Godalming Binscombe, and Haslemere East and Grayswood wards, and for three councillors to be elected for the Hindhead ward.

Notes:

 [1] 1992 c. 19. Section 17 is amended by section 39(4) and (5) of the Police and Magistrates' Courts Act 1994 (c. 29).back

[2] S.I. 1999/2482.back
