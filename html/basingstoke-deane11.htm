Local Election Results 2011

Basingstoke and Deane

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Brighton Hill South, Brookvale and Kings Furlong, Buckskin, East Woodhay, Popley West, Rooksdown, Sherborne St John or Whitchurch. The election in Popley East this year was a by-election.
