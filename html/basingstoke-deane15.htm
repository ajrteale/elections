Local Election Results 2015

Basingstoke and Deane

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Brighton Hill South, Brookvale and Kings Furlong, Buckskin, East Woodhay, Popley East, Popley West, Sherborne St John or Whitchurch. The election in Rooksdown this year was a by-election.
