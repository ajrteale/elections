Local Election Results 2007

Basingstoke and Deane

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Baughurst, Brighton Hill North, Brighton Hill South, Burghclere, East Woodhay, Eastrop, Highclere and Bourne, Pamber, Rooksdown or Sherborne St John. One Conservative councillor was elected unopposed.
