Local Election Results 2006

Daventry

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Abbey South, Badby, Boughton and Pitsford, Braunston, Clipston, Crick, Flore, Ravensthorpe, Walgrave, Weedon or Welford. Six Conservative councillors were elected unopposed.
