Local Election Results 2003

Daventry

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Barby and Kilsby, Boughton and Pitsford, Brampton, Braunston, Byfield, Flore, Ravensthorpe, Spratton, West Haddon and Guilsborough, Woodford or Yelvertoft. One Conservative councillor was elected unopposed.
