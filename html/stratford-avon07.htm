Local Election Results 2007

Stratford-on-Avon

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Aston Cantlow, Brailes, Burton Dassett, Claverdon, Ettington, Kinwarton, Long Compton, Long Itchington, Quinton, Sambourne, Snitterfield, Tanworth or Vale of the Red Horse. One Liberal Democrat councillor was elected unopposed. The elections in Bardon and Harbury this year were by-elections.
