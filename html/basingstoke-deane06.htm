Local Election Results 2006

Basingstoke and Deane

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Baughurst, Buckskin, Calleva, Popley West, Rooksdown, Sherborne St John, Tadley North, Tadley South or Upton Grey and The Candovers. The election in Popley East this year was a by-election.
