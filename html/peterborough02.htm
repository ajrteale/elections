Local Election Results 2002

Peterborough

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Barnack, North, South, South Bretton or Walton.

Note:— These elections were to renew the councillors elected to a three-year term in 1999. The 2003 election in Peterborough was postponed to 2004.
