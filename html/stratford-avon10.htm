Local Election Results 2010

Stratford-on-Avon

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Aston Cantlow, Bardon, Burton Dassett, Claverdon, Ettington, Fenny Compton, Kineton, Stockton and Napton, Stratford Mount Pleasant, Tredington or Welford. The elections in Henley and Shipston this year were by-elections.
