Local Election Results 2006

Stratford-on-Avon

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Aston Cantlow, Bardon, Burton Dassett, Claverdon, Ettington, Fenny Compton, Kineton, Shipston, Stockton and Napton, Stratford Mount Pleasant, Tredington or Welford. One Independent councillor was elected unopposed. The election in Henley this year was a by-election.
