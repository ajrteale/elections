Local Election Results 2008

Castle Point

One-third of council up for election. No election this year in Canvey Island West.

CIIP = Canvey Island Independent Party

Note:— There was a by-election in Canvey Island South which, unusually, was held on a separate ballot paper rather than combined with the main council election. The main council election result is in the main list below; the result of the by-election was:

Canvey Island South by-election

Janice Payne CIIP 930 55.6%
Margaret Belford C 596 35.6%
John Payne Lab 146 8.7%
