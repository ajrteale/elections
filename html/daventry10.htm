Local Election Results 2010

Daventry

One-third of council up for election. No elections this year in Abbey South, Badby, Boughton and Pitsford, Braunston, Clipston, Crick, Flore, Ravensthorpe, Walgrave, Weedon or Welford. One Conservative councillor was elected unopposed.

EDP = English Democrats Party
