Local elections 2022

Islington's local council elections took place on Thursday 5 May 2022.

Statement of persons nominated

You can see all of the candidates who contested the May 2022 local council elections in the 'Statement of Persons Nominated' document under 'Useful documents'.

Results

As a result of the May 2022 elections, Islington has 48 Labour party councillors and 3 Green party councillors.

Out of 148,227 registered voters there were 53,377 votes cast. Which is around a 36% turnout. There were a total of 289 rejected votes.

Please select the ward you are interested in to see the full results and who has been elected. Three councillors are elected to represent each ward.
