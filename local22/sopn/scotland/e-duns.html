Local Government Elections - May 2022

Local Election

On 5 May 2022 the constituents of East Dunbartonshire will be invited to vote to elect their local Councillor, 22 Councillors across the 7 wards of East Dunbartonshire. In this election voters will be asked to mark their preferences on the ballot paper - 1 for their first choice, 2 for their second preferred candidate, 3 for the third and so on. Voters can mark their preferences for as many or as few as they like.

Prospective Candidates

Please find attached the East Dunbartonshire nomination packs for each ward. Completed nominations can be submitted from 10am on Tuesday 15 March 2022 with the nomination process running until 4pm on Wednesday 30 March 2022. Prospective Candidates should submit their nominations for an informal check and should then make an appointment to submit their nomination papers between 10am and 4pm weekdays. Nomination packs for each Ward are attached below.

Please note, the candidate packs can take a few minutes to open. If after this time there are any issues please contact the email address below.
