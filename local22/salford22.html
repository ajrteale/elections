Election results

New government requirement to show ID at polling stations

The last Local Government elections took place on Thursday 5 May 2022 and this is where the results will appear for the next local election on Thursday 4 May 2023.

Below are the results from the last election.

The count for the local election took place at the AJ Bell Stadium, 1 Stadium Way, Eccles, M30 7EY.

Salford City Council comprises 20 electoral wards with three councillors representing each ward.

The city council is controlled by the Labour party, the political composition of the city council is now:

Party Councillors
Labour 49
Conservative 8
Liberal Democrat 2
Independent 1
