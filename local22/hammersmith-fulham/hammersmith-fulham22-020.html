2022 White City ward result

Result of the election of 3 councillors held on 5 May 2022 in White City ward.

Result of the election for THREE councillors in White City ward held on Thursday 5 May 2022.

The results were declared at 04:27 on Friday 6 May 2022 and the turnout was 27.5%.

The candidates duly elected to serve as councillors for White City ward were:

  • JONES Andrew Malcom (Lab)
  • PEREZ Natalia (Lab)
  • UMEH Frances (Lab)
