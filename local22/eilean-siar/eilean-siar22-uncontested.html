Declaration Of Uncontested Election

Election of Councillors - Thursday 5 May 2022

Ward 1: Barraigh agus Bhatarsaigh;

Ward 6: Sgìr’ Ùige agus Carlabhagh; and

Ward 11: Sgìre an Rubha

I, Malcolm Burr, Returning Officer for the Election of Councillors to Comhairle nan Eilean Siar, give notice that the candidates named below were validly nominated for the electoral wards named above and, as the number of candidates nominated for each ward was equal to or less than the number of Councillors to be returned, no poll took place. In accordance with paragraph 12 of the Scottish Local Government Elections Rules, the candidates named below are hereby declared to be elected as Councillors for Comhairle nan Eilean Siar, representing the wards named below.

Declaration Of Uncontested Election
Electoral Ward Name of Candidate Elected Description
Ward 1: Barraigh agus Bhatarsaigh Kenneth John MacLean Independent
Ward 6: Sgìr’ Ùige agus Carlabhagh Ranald Fraser Scottish Conservative and Unionist
Ward 11: Sgìre an Rubha

Norrie Tomsh MacDonald

 Independent
Ward 11: Sgìre an Rubha Finlay Mackenzie Stewart Independent

Signed

Malcolm Burr
Returning Officer
Comhairle nan Eilean Siar
