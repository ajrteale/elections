Local Election 2022: Results

Local Election 2022: Results held on 5 May 2022

Last updated: 9 May 2023

Next review: 9 May 2024

Elections took place in all 22 of the borough’s wards with two or three councillors elected in each ward to give us our total of 60 councillors.

Turnout was 34.36%. The breakdown of elected members is: 

  • Labour - 47 members (increase of one on results from previous elections in 2018)
  • Conservative - 13 members (decrease of one)
