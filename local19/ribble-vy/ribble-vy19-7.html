Borough Elections 2019 - Results

Dilworth Ward

An Election was held on 2nd May 2019 for Dilworth Ward of Ribble Valley Borough Council.

The result is set out below:

  • Turnout: 31.31%
  • Electorate: 2,031

For more details please see the Declaration Result of Poll.

Name Party Votes cast  
GEE, Harold Labour Party 203  
HOLDEN, Brian Joseph Conservative Party 402 ELECTED
HUMPHREYS, Angeline Conservative Party 356 ELECTED
STUBBS, Nicholas Labour Party 202  
    17 Spoilt Vote  

Brian Joseph Holden and Angeline Humphreys were duly elected councillor for the Dilworth Ward of Ribble Valley Borough Council.

Marshal Scott
Returning Officer
