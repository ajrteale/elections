Local council election results

There are 33 councillors in Harlow, 3 for each of the 11 wards.

One third of the council is re-elected every year, with the exception of years in which county council elections take place.

The council is controlled by Labour. The political makeup of the council is 20 Labour, 13 Conservative.

Results

The last local elections were held on 2 May 2019.

You can look at the full declaration of results for the local elections 2019 (pdf)
