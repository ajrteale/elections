Local elections to appoint City of York councillors for council wards in York were held on Thursday 2 May 2019. Details of elected candidates were announced by Returning Officer, Mary Weastell, on Friday 3 May.

Outcomes of the local election

Following the results of the local elections 'final seats' on City of York Council, by party are:

  • Conservative Party: 2
  • Labour Party: 17
  • Liberal Democrat Party: 21
  • Green Party: 4
  • Independent: 3
  • UK Independence Party: 0
  • Socialist Alternative Party: 0
  • Women's Equality Party: 0

24 or more seats are needed for the majority.
