Borough Council Election results 2019

  • Print
  • Email

The Conservatives have retained control of Erewash Borough Council with 27 seats. Labour won 19 seats and Liberal Democrats won one seat.

Here are the full results of the Erewash Borough Council elections 2019 which took place on 2 May.

Elected members are highlighted in bold. 

The turnout was 34 per cent.
