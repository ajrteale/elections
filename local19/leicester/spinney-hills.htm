Spinney Hills

The results for Spinney Hills ward have been declared. Misbah Batool (Labour) and Ghulam Mustafa Malik (Labour) are elected. Turnout was 48.14%.

Further information

Candidate Party Votes
Mehboob Akuji The Conservative Party Candidate 934
Bob Ball The Green Party 215
Misbah Batool Labour Party 2847
Ghulam Mustafa Malik Labour Party 2932
Brenner Jack Munden Liberal Democrats 138
Inayat Patel The Conservative Party Candidate 944
Drew Walton Socialist Alternative 79
