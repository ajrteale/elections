Christmas and New Year services

Find out about our bin collections over Christmas and new year.

Due to Covid-19 our offices remain closed to the public until further notice. Our phone lines will be closed from 1pm on Friday 24 December until 8.30am on Tuesday 4 January. You can still pay for services using our 24/7 phone payment line 03300 882834. You can report issues or request information using our contact us form. We will respond to any enquiries when staff return in January. 

In an emergency please call our out of hours number on 0330 678 2382

For the latest coronavirus advice and guidance please visit the government coronavirus website.
