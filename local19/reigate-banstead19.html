Election results - Friday 3 May 2019

Elections for Borough Councillors took place on Thursday 2 May 2019. All wards and all seats were up for election. This meant electors could vote for three candidates.

The seats for Horley Town Council and Salfords and Sidlow Parish Council were also up for election on Thursday 2 May 2019.

Register to Vote

To vote in elections you must be registered to vote . The deadline to register to vote in the Borough Council Elections is midnight Friday 12 April 2019.

Applications to vote by post or change/cancel an existing postal or proxy vote must be received by 5pm Monday 15 April 2019.

New applications to vote by proxy must be received by 5pm Wednesday 24 April 2019.

If you are registered and eligible to vote in these elections, you will receive a poll card around the first week of April informing you where your polling station is, or if you've applied for a postal vote, when you can expect to receive your postal voting pack.

If you are not sure whether you are registered to vote you can call the elections helpline number on 01737 276794.

You can find out more about voting in Borough Council elections and other elections being held on 2 May 2019 at Your Vote Matters.

Electoral wards

Following the local government boundary changes the Borough will be divided into 15 wards from 2 May 2019.

The Council will have 45 elected Members (3 councillors per ward) following this election. This link provides further information on our Councillors.

On 2 May 2019 all council seats will be up for election. Electors will be voting for 3 candidates for each ward.

By-elections

By-elections can be held at any time if a vacancy arises on the council.

For more information please email elreg@reigate-banstead.gov.uk .
