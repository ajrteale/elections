Worthing Borough elections results:
Thursday 2nd May 2019

Note: There are no elections in the Durrington and Northbrook wards this year

All the results for the Worthing Borough Council 2019 elections are now in.

11 of the 37 seats were up for election with Conservatives winning 5, Labour 5 and Liberal Democrats 1.
