District Election 2015

Summary

On this page you can find results for the election held on 7 May 2015. This election was for every ward on Uttlesford District Council.

The following information is displayed for each ward: the number of District Councillors to be elected (seats), the candidates' names, the parties they represent, the number of votes they received, the number of ballot papers that were rejected, the size of the electorate and the percentage turnout this represented.

The candidates whose names are in bold won seats on Uttlesford District Council.
