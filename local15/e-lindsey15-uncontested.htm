ELECTION 2015: Local district council candidates confirmed

12:10 13:17 Friday 10 April 2015
0
Have your say

The candidates fighting for borough wards in Boston and East Lindsey have now been confirmed.

Borough and parish councillors will be elected at the same time as the General Election candidates.

Coastal – Tom Ashton (Con), Peter Bedford (Con), Felicity Ransom (UKIP).
