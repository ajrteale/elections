Election 2015: Waverley Borough Council candidates

  • 12:06, 20 Apr 2015
  • Updated 14:11, 24 Apr 2015
  • By David Stubbings

We've put together a list of all candidates looking to be elected onto Waverley Borough Council in this year's local elections

  •   Shares

Get daily news by email

As well as the general election, residents in Waverley will also have the chance to vote for their new councillors on Thursday May 7.

Candidates from parties such as the Conservative, Labour, Liberal Democrats and the Green Party will take on representatives of minor groups and independents.

Polls will be open from 7am-10pm.

Votes will be counted at Edge Leisure Centre, Woolmer Hill Road, Haslemere, from 1pm on Friday May 8 with a result expected between 6pm and 7pm.
