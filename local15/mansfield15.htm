EU Referendum results June 2016

On Thursday 23 June 2016, residents of Mansfield took part in a national referendum to determine whether Britain shall remain or leave the European Union.

Please follow us on Twitter MDC_News or on our My Mansfield Facebook page for updates about the count. All the results will be published here as soon as possible after they are declared.

If you are a journalist with an urgent media enquiry about the referendum, please contact 07904 154129 or 07946 335953. If you have a media enquiry about any other matter, please contact the Press Office on 01623 463021 during office hours.
