Borough Elections 2015

The next borough and parish elections will be held on Thursday 7 May 2015. Along with the borough and parish elections there will also be a Parliamentary Election on 7 May 2015.

In order to vote in the Parliamentary General election and Local elections, you must be registered to vote. If you are not registered to vote, register to vote now.

All notices and information relating to the borough election will be posted on this page. For information and notices relating to the parish election click here.

Results will be announced on this page following the local and parish count on Friday 8 May after 1pm.

Click the required ward for notices and results when available.
