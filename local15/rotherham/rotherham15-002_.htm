Brinsworth and Catcliffe Ward

Election of a Borough Councillor for Brinsworth and Catcliffe Ward on Thursday 7 May 2015

I, Jacqueline Collins, being the Returning Officer at this election, do hereby give notice that the number of votes recorded for each Candidate is as follows:

 Name of Candidate  Description (if any)  Number of Votes*
 MEHBAN Omar  The Conservative Party Candidate  684
 RODDISON Andrew Scott  Labour Party  2,744 ELECTED
 SAUNDERSON Ian  UK Independence Party (UKIP)  2,318

* If elected the word 'Elected' appears against the number of votes.

The number of ballot papers rejected was as follows: Number of ballot papers
 A  Want of an official mark  0
 B  Voting for more Candidates than the voter was entitled to  5
 C  Writing or mark by which the voter could be identified  1
 D  Being unmarked or wholly void for uncertainty  18
Total  24
 Vacant seats  1  Electorate  9,444  Ballot papers issued  5,770  Turnout  61.09%

I do hereby declare that the said Andrew Scott Roddison is duly elected Councillor for the Brinsworth and Catcliffe Ward.

Jacqueline Collins

Returning Officer
