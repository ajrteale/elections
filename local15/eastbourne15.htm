Borough Council Elections 2015

Borough Council Elections 2015

The results of the Borough Council elections, held on Thursday 7th May 2015 are available below, ward by ward.

Result: Conservatives 9 seats (-3), Liberal Democrats 18 seats (+3)

Liberal Democrats retained control of Eastbourne Borough Council.
