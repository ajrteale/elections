UPDATE: Chichester District Council full election results

19:27 Friday 08 May 2015
1
Have your say

THE votes have been counted and the results for the Chichester District Council election are in.

Here are all the results from the district council election across the district:

The final tally of seats is Tories 42, Lib Dems 3, Inds 3

Bosham (two seats): Myles Cullen (Con) 1426, Gillian Gardiner (Lib Dem) 945, Penny Plant (Con) 1506

ELECTED: Myles Cullen, Penny Plant

Tories hold both seats

Bury (one seat): John Elliott (Con) 970, David Hares (Lib Dem) 376

ELECTED: John Elliott, Tory hold

Chichester East (three seats): Quentin Cox (Lib Dem) 1184, Thomas Dempster (Con) 1321, Ben Earnshaw-Mansell (Lab) 1044, Tony French (Lib Dem) 1161, Les Hixson (Con) 1214, Jane Kilby (Con) 1381, Michael Woolley (Lib Dem) 1040

ELECTED: Thomas Dempster, Les Hixson and Jane Kilby

Tory take all three seats

ELECTED: Peter Budge, Tony Dignum and Simon Lloyd-Williams

Tories hold all three seats

ELECTED: Pam Dignum, Nigel Galloway, Len Macey
